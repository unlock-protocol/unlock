<h2 id="h-background" class="text-3xl font-header">Background</h2><p>Since last year, Unlock Protocol DAO has been in conversations with ICHI Finance and ApeBond to launch a campaign together to get the $UP token into more hands. As you might know, the $UP tokens' primary purpose is <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://docs.unlock-protocol.com/governance/unlock-dao/">governance</a> and therefore the medium of control over changes to the actual protocol technology (not the website, but how the technology you can access through the website works). Now that the campaign with ApeBond is live, we want to take a moment to explain not just what‚Äôs happening, but why it matters and why your involvement is important in this journey.</p><h2 id="h-why-are-we-doing-this" class="text-3xl font-header">Why Are We Doing This?</h2><p>Unlock Protocol was created with a simple but powerful idea: users should own and govern the technology they use and be in control of their own data. Our $UP token is a tool for this, giving people a real say in how the protocol grows and changes. But for this to work, $UP must be in the hands of many, not just a few.</p><p>That‚Äôs where ApeBond comes in. By working with them, we hope to make it easier for more people to become part of Unlock‚Äôs story and to have a say in where we go next.</p><h2 id="h-what-is-apebond" class="text-3xl font-header">What Is ApeBond?</h2><p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="http://apebond.click/UP">ApeBond</a> is a platform that helps projects like ours share tokens with new communities fairly and transparently. Instead of quick sales or hype, ApeBond uses a ‚Äúbonding‚Äù process: people can exchange stablecoins or other assets for $UP, which is released gradually over time. This approach is designed to encourage long-term participation, not just speculation. Additionally, the token is offered at a discounted price, making it more accessible to a broader diversity of people. </p><p>Please review the documentation for more insights here so that you can do your own research, which is what we always encourage you to do.</p><h2 id="h-why-does-open-peer-governed-tech-matter" class="text-3xl font-header">Why Does Open, Peer-Governed Tech Matter?</h2><p>It‚Äôs easy to take for granted that distant companies control the apps we use every day. Instead of a self-serving technology giant, we would have a genuine technology we could truly rely on!</p><p>Open source, peer-governed projects like Unlock Protocol offer a different path:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Transparency:</strong> Anyone can see how things work and suggest improvements through our governance process on the <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://discord.gg/v8wFYQ6DVu">Discord Forum</a> and <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://www.tally.xyz/gov/unlock-protocol">our onchain voting platform via Tally</a>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Collective Ownership:</strong> Decisions are made together by everyone <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://docs.unlock-protocol.com/governance/unlock-dao/">who holds the $UP Token</a>, not handed down from above.</p></li><li><p><strong>Resilience:</strong> When many people care and contribute, the project is stronger. <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out"
