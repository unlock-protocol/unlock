<p>What makes it worth spending your free time contributing to a DAO? Is it the token price, the amazingly written whitepaper, or the never-ending roadmap? It is neither, specifically not in Unlock Protocol DAO, even when the market was not in the messy situation it is currently in. <strong>The people who decided to show up</strong> do it because we know a protocol needs people who understand the importance of decentralized technology and build amazing things on top of it.</p><p>That's what Unlock Protocol DAO members have been doing quietly, consistently, and across multiple continents. Here's a look at some of the community contributions that have been moving the needle, and why I believe they matter beyond the governance forum or Discord chats.</p><hr><h2 id="h-from-volunteerism-to-a-real-contributor-economy" class="text-3xl font-header !mt-8 !mb-4 first:!mt-0 first:!mb-0"><strong>From Volunteerism to a Real Contributor Economy</strong></h2><p>For a long time, DAOs operated on goodwill. People contributed because they <strong>believed in the mission, but recognition, if not compensation, is needed</strong> for sustainability reasons. <strong>Unlock DAO officially changed that</strong> with the approval of the&nbsp;<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://www.tally.xyz/gov/unlock-protocol/proposal/4690288635831369650578024132446863170116166875528511300664433815615415133450"><strong>DAO Rewards System</strong></a>, an onchain framework that recognizes contributors fairly and transparently by granting token ownership to the protocol's governance.</p><p>By the way, contributions in Unlock DAO can look very different depending on your skills and future goals. Currently, you can:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Contribute to the </strong><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://github.com/unlock-protocol/unlock/issues"><strong>GitHub Repo</strong></a>. We have several bounties up and problems that need fixing, from tiny to medium.</p></li><li><p><strong>Contribute to the Marketing &amp; Communication</strong> team under the Stewardship of <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/cecilia-contreras-castro/"><strong>Ceci Sakura</strong></a>. Add ideas and concrete content to our <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/15IDZa01zP-adp6yhwhLx22P1fH_meRYmFQwd5rk-dQo/edit?usp=sharing"><strong>interim Spreadsheet here</strong></a>. Stay tuned for our upcoming collab with <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://incented.co/"><strong>Incented</strong></a> by subscribing to this newsletter!</p></li><li><p><strong>And lastly: Built with Unlock.</strong> What needs to be solved that Unlock can help with? Unlock is open-source, and our <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow ugc" class="dont-break-out" href="https://docs.unlock-protocol.com/"><strong>docs</strong></a> can help you with your ideas.</p></li></ul><p>The road to the heart of a DAO is versatile, and no one way needs to be yours. Mine has been on every end of this spectrum, and I have enjoyed every instance of it!</p><hr><h2 id="h-real-projects-real-adoption" class="text-3xl font-header !mt-8 !mb-4 first:!mt-0 first:!mb-0"><strong>Real Projects. Real Adoption.</strong></h2><p>Let's dive a bit deeper into the last way to contribute to Unlock Protoco DAO: actually building with it, because the best proof of Unlock's value isn't a case study, it's a minted key.
